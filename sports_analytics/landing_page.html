Sports Analytics



About

These projects are various sports-related projects that I have worked on. Personally, I really enjoy baseball, football, and hockey, and I'm a huge Boston/New England fan (which is one of the reasons I chose the image on the right for this page).



Projects:

Baseball Game Day Map (2022)
 Key Idea: If you were to plan a baseball roadtrip (like I did), where could you go? This shows all major and minor league games by day. You can also select a team you like to see where they are playing each day.
 Note: The data on this page was last updated on March 29, 2022, so some games may have been rescheduled/moved.

Revamping the NFL Playoff System (2020)
 Key Idea: In the NFL, the best 6 (now 7) teams don't always make the playoffs. Let's fix that.

MLB Double Play Tracker (2019)
 Key Question: Which combinations of MLB double plays have occurred? A 1-8-6? A 6-7-5?
 Note: This page is hosted on Heroku, and may take up to a minute to load.
